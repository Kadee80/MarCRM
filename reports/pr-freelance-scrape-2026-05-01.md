This is consistent with the previous week's scrape and confirms the structural reality of this pipeline: the openings Mark wants are *implied capacity gaps*, not explicit freelance postings. No US-based B2B fintech Series B/C raises with comms-greenfield potential hit the wire in the window. Expect the daily scrape to continue carrying the funding-news lead generation.

Worth treating "PR agency hiring senior leadership" as a recurring high-priority signal for this pipeline.
