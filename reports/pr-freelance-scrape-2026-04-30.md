## Trends in PR Hiring Market (window 2026-04-23 → 2026-04-30)

1. **Big-tech / consumer comms postings still dominate** — Visa (Sr. Director Brand Comms, 4/28), Coinbase (Head of Comms, Base, posted 3/21 still active), and similar large-cap comms postings are visible but are weak fractional/freelance fits. They're full-time strategic hires with multi-year mandates and existing comms departments. Suppressed from this report.

2. **Agency hiring at the senior level is the strongest 4/2026 signal** — 5WPR's VP B2B Tech is the cleanest agency-overflow signal of the week. Mid-tier B2B PR agencies have been visibly capacity-constrained throughout Q1 2026 and the senior-leadership postings (vs. junior account-exec postings) suggest revenue overhang at the partner book level — the kind of overflow agencies pay for in cash, not equity. Worth a separate sweep next cycle of: Walker Sands, Bospar, BMV, Inkhouse, Highwire, Mission North.

3. **"First PR hire" postings are scarce in this window** — most freelance-fit signals are coming from POST-funding announcements where comms hasn't been hired yet (HockeyStack, Courier Health), rather than from explicit "we need our first PR person" job postings. Mark should treat the Crunchbase / TechCrunch funding feed as the higher-yield input for first-PR-hire intent than LinkedIn Jobs filtering.

4. **Fractional/freelance PR job board volume is up but quality is down** — Toptal, Upwork, Fractional Jobs, FlexJobs all show steady volume but heavily weighted toward consumer / DTC / individual-influencer work. Almost no fintech / legal / PE-portfolio postings on the freelance-explicit boards. Mark's freelance pipeline will continue to come from inferring intent from full-time postings + fundraises, NOT from explicit freelance listings.

5. **Healthtech post-funding comms is an underserved adjacency** — Courier Health is one of three Oak HC/FT post-Series-B leads in the past 30 days where the company doesn't have an in-house comms function. If Mark wants to expand sector reach, Oak HC/FT and General Catalyst Health portfolios are warm lists.
