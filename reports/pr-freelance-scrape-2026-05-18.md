Two months ago Mark would have had to surface these via outbound; now they're showing up indexed.

The Work Crowd is already in MarCRM from the 2026-05-08 scrape; Hanson Search and Forthright Advising are worth a separate "channel partner" tracking treatment rather than treating them as leads.
