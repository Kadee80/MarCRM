## Trends in the legal freelance market

The fractional/interim GC market continues to be the most reliable source of legitimate freelance demand, but the postings increasingly cluster around generalist operating-company coverage (commercial contracts, risk, compliance) rather than fund work — both Grade B leads this run fit that pattern. Genuine fund-formation freelance demand is more concentrated: it shows up through ALSP networks (Ontra, Ropes & Gray's remote funds program, Axiom) rather than as standalone dated reqs, which means it scores lower on recency but offers more durable, repeatable volume. Published comp remains the exception — only the staffing-firm and ALSP postings tend to quote bands; direct operating-company fractional roles almost never do, which is why `comp_credible` keeps scoring 0 on this lead type. Fully remote is now effectively the default for fractional and freelance counsel work; it is no longer a differentiator.
